Italy

Italy is the fifth most visited country in the world, welcoming some 46 million foreign visitors annually. It’s also the fifth most populous country in Europe. Another interesting fact about Italy, the capital city of Rome is almost 3,000 years old.

Cambodia

The largest religious structure ever built is Angkor Wat (City Temple) in Cambodia. The site measures a massive 162.6 hectares and was built by the Khmer King Suryavarman II from 1113-50 to honor the Hindu god Vishnu.

Belgium

The Big Bang theory originated in Belgium – it was a Belgian, the priest and physicist (apparently not a contradiction in terms) Georges Lemaitre, who first came up with what is now called the Big Bang theory of the origin of the universe in 1927.

Mexico

The first great civilization in Mexico were the Olmecs (1400-300 B.C.) who established many cities along the eastern coast of Mexico, sculpted the famous Colossal Heads, and worshipped a mysterious, unnamed god that was part human and part jaguar.
